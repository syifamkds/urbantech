About Us

Based in Bandung, Indonesia, we have the advantage of working primarily with small to medium sized businesses. Our passion is to help your business to achieve its marketing goal; helping you gain new businesses and attract potential customers by creating engaging and exciting marketing tools.


We focus on technology based marketing strategies that work together seamlessly to help your company reach your audience. We use traditional media (print, video) as well as web design, social media marketing, blogs and SEO (search engine optimization) combined with marketing strategies that will help achieve your business goals.
